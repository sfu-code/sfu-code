Rome wasn't built in a day.

But when motivated people come together, impressive things happen. That's why we're starting the SFU Software Club.

What is this?

SFU Code Club is a new club starting in Fall 2018. We aim to bring together all kinds of developers so knowledge can be shared, connections can be made, and projects can be built.

Our topics include:

React
Laravel
AWS
Swift
Java
Jekyll
AWS
Swift

Club Mandate

To bring together students interested in the design and development of digital products and services, fostering a community of practice where students can learn from others and share their own knowledge.
